How to add to Gmail
1
Fill in your details above, then click Copy signature.
2
Open Gmail Settings — click the gear icon (⚙) → See all settings.
3
Scroll to the Signature section. Create a new signature or select your existing one to edit.
4
Click inside the signature text box, then paste (Cmd+V on Mac / Ctrl+V on Windows). The formatted signature should appear — not code.
5
In the Signature defaults section just below, set your new signature in both the For new emails use and On reply/forward use dropdowns.
6
Click Save Changes at the bottom of the page. Done!
